WHAT WE DO

There’s a type of shed for every purpose.You imagine, we’ll take care of the construction. Each space is tailored just for you.

Modern-Shed was born from builders and dreamers—idealists and pragmatic problem-solvers. We build intentionally crafted backyard studios, by connecting the dots between form and function. Our answer to living in a limited space lies a few steps outside your door, whether it’s a home office tucked back by the garden, a guest room decorated with sunlight, a dedicated place of movement and meditation, or a studio meant for photography, writing, painting, or any interest that doesn’t quite fit across the kitchen table. In our endeavor to create a thoughtful addition to your home, we bring in our cost-effective and design-friendly expertise, rather than opting for a costly renovation. We could all use a little more space to grow—let it be created specifically for you, a room of possibility and plenty.

The Difference

(A) In typical construction, it takes an all-hands-on-deck approach. Your project might require a building designer, architect, structural engineer, or a general contractor, leading to not only a high price tag, but a yard that is transformed into a construction zone. From sawdust to large scraps to trampled landscaping, we’re seeking to step right on over the mess.
(B) With Modern-Shed, pre-frabricated, carry-by-hand panels arrive at your doorstep. The materials assemble quickly and easily by your handy-person or one of our install teams with minimal waste and clean up. Battah-bing, battah-boom. Let’s make it even easier: panels are constructed indoors so your lumber isn’t sitting out in the rain for weeks. And not to brag, but our materials are the best of the best in terms of quality.
